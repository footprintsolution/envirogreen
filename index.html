Ideology of e-nviroGreen

We, at e-nviroGreen, believe and understand tomorrow, today. This thought process drives us in each and every aspect of our action by looking ahead of times and the proven technology based innovation philosophy. We work irrationally, in alignment with the objectives of the client without compromising with our own work philosophy of not building just for the sake of building but by playing with the innovative materials, resources and state-of-the-art tools and technology. We, not only focus on doing it ourselves, but also strive to create awareness and educate the industry to adopt towards the required change in conventional thought process. We understand sustainability as a revolution which needs to be brought into the world, proudly and happily taking it as a responsibility on our young and experienced shoulders. We believe in eliminating “possibility blindness”, a widely-spread disease which restricts the architects and engineers to visualize beyond the normal and conventional.

The United Nation defines Sustainability in Seventeen different parameters, which is much beyond the construction industry only. The design philosophy and methodology of e-nviroGreen gives due consideration to all these parameters achieving holistic sustainable development goals specially through the CSR initiatives.

We believe in sustainable action and it starts with us.
